# Voice & Style Card

*A one-page capture of how you sound and look. The assistant fills this in from your Design Interview and saves it to memory, then applies it to everything it writes or builds for you. Keep it short. One page that the assistant reads every time beats ten pages it ignores.*

## Voice (how I write)

- **Register:** [formal ↔ conversational — where you sit]
- **Person:** [we / I / neither]
- **Sentence style:** [short & punchy / considered & flowing]
- **Signature phrases:** [words and phrases you actually use]
- **Never say:** [clichés, buzzwords, and tones to avoid]
- **Sounds like me:** [1 to 2 short samples of your real writing]

## Feeling (the impression to leave)

- **Should feel:** [3 to 5 adjectives — e.g., confident, clear, modern]
- **Should never feel:** [e.g., salesy, generic, cold, chaotic]

## Aesthetic (how I look)

- **Adjectives:** [5 to 8 words for the visual feel]
- **Base:** [light / dark]
- **Density:** [spare & calm ↔ rich & busy — target on the Quietness Spectrum]
- **Shape language:** [sharp & geometric / soft & rounded]

## Foundations (filled from the design system)

- **Colors:** [primary, accents, neutrals]
- **Fonts:** [headline / body]
- **Admire:** [a reference look you like] · **Avoid:** [a look you dislike]

## Audience

- **Primary reader:** [one real person — role, what they care about]
- **Register of the audience:** [expert / executive / newcomer / dual]

---

*Usage rule for the assistant: apply this card to all content unless told otherwise. When a request conflicts with the card, follow the card and flag the conflict.*
